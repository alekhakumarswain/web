Technical Club

The Technical Club at College of Engineering Bhubaneswar is dedicated to fostering technical skills and innovation among students. From organizing workshops on the latest technologies to participating in coding competitions, our club provides ample opportunities for students to enhance their technical expertise.

Members of the Technical Club have access to state-of-the-art facilities and resources, along with guidance from experienced faculty members. Join us to explore the fascinating world of technology and unleash your creative potential!

Sports Club

The Sports Club at College of Engineering Bhubaneswar is dedicated to promoting physical fitness and sportsmanship among students. Our club offers a wide range of sports activities, including cricket, football, volleyball, basketball, and badminton.

Members of the Sports Club have the opportunity to participate in inter-college tournaments, friendly matches, and recreational sports events. Join us to stay active, healthy, and connected with fellow sports enthusiasts!

Cultural Club

The Cultural Club at College of Engineering Bhubaneswar celebrates diversity and creativity through various cultural events and activities. From traditional dance performances to modern music concerts, our club provides a platform for students to showcase their talents and express themselves.

Members of the Cultural Club have the opportunity to participate in cultural festivals, talent shows, and cultural exchange programs. Join us to explore different cultures, make lifelong friendships, and create memorable experiences!

Social & Welfare Club

The Social & Welfare Club at College of Engineering Bhubaneswar is committed to making a positive impact on society through various community service initiatives and welfare programs. Our club organizes blood donation drives, health camps, cleanliness campaigns, and charity fundraisers to address social issues and contribute to the welfare of the community.

Members of the Social & Welfare Club have the opportunity to volunteer for meaningful projects, collaborate with NGOs, and make a difference in the lives of others. Join us to be a part of something bigger and create a better world for everyone!
